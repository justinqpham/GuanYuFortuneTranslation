# Guan Yu Fortune Sticks - Batch 03 (Sticks 21-30)

STICK #21
===========================================

### ENGLISH TRANSLATION

Poor Fortune (Unfavorable / Caution)

**Sacred Meaning:**
- **Health:** seek divine resolution
- **Wealth:** guard and report harm
- **Reputation:** litigation must be resolved
- **Travel / Movement:** affairs not successful
- **Marriage:** illness anxious
- **Misfortune:** without approval
- **Blessings:** affairs are not suitable
- **Litigation:** there is truth

**Fortune Poem:**
With you in the past, a grudge was formed,
Today when meeting, the evil fate is revealed,
Recite scriptures and chant sutras abundantly,
Pray to protect the women within your household.

**Interpretation:**
• This fortune warns about unresolved conflicts from the past resurfacing. "Grudge was formed" indicates old enmities or betrayals that haven't healed, and current encounters will trigger these painful dynamics.
• The danger is specifically directed at women in the household ("protect the women"). This suggests vulnerability in family relationships or that female family members may become targets of vindictive behavior.
• The remedy is spiritual protection through prayer and scripture. Worldly strategies won't resolve karmic debts—only cultivation of virtue, recitation of sacred texts, and divine protection can neutralize the threat. Avoid confrontation and seek higher guidance.

### VIETNAMESE TRANSLATION

Hạ Hạ (Không thuận / Cần đề phòng)

**Sacred Meaning:**
- **Sức khỏe:** tìm kiếm giải quyết thần linh
- **Tài lộc:** bảo vệ và báo cáo tổn hại
- **Danh tiếng:** kiện tụng phải được giải quyết
- **Người đi xa / Di chuyển:** công việc không thành công
- **Hôn nhân:** bệnh lo lắng
- **Tai họa:** không có sự chấp thuận
- **Phúc lộc:** công việc không thích hợp
- **Kiện tụng:** có sự thật

**Bài thơ quẻ:**
Với bạn trong quá khứ, một mối hận đã hình thành,
Hôm nay khi gặp gỡ, duyên xấu xa được tiết lộ,
Tụng kinh và niệm chú dồi dào,
Cầu nguyện để bảo vệ phụ nữ trong gia đình bạn.

**Ý nghĩa:**
• Quẻ này cảnh báo về xung đột chưa được giải quyết từ quá khứ nổi lên bề mặt. "Mối hận đã hình thành" chỉ ra những thù hận hoặc sự phản bội cũ chưa lành, và những cuộc gặp gỡ hiện tại sẽ kích hoạt những động lực đau đớn này.
• Nguy hiểm được hướng cụ thể vào phụ nữ trong gia đình ("bảo vệ phụ nữ"). Điều này gợi ý sự dễ bị tổn thương trong quan hệ gia đình hoặc các thành viên nữ trong gia đình có thể trở thành mục tiêu của hành vi trả thù.
• Phương thuốc là bảo vệ tinh thần thông qua cầu nguyện và kinh văn. Chiến lược thế gian sẽ không giải quyết được nợ nghiệp—chỉ có tu dưỡng đức hạnh, tụng kinh văn thiêng và sự bảo vệ thần linh mới có thể vô hiệu hóa mối đe dọa. Tránh đối đầu và tìm kiếm hướng dẫn cao hơn.

### ENGLISH TRANSLATION

Upper Fortune (Good / Auspicious)

**Sacred Meaning:**
- **Health:** illness is immediately relieved
- **Wealth:** financial success is abundant
- **Reputation:** easily achieved
- **Travel / Movement:** litigation decided and victorious
- **Marriage:** marriage suitable
- **Misfortune:** giving birth to a noble son
- **Blessings:** all affairs successfully handled
- **Litigation:** family path accomplished

**Fortune Poem:**
In the jade pond unfolds the white lotus,
Its natural color appears distinctly from heaven,
A lifetime of bone structure surpasses the ordinary,
Proper and settled people ask about the number one immortal.

**Interpretation:**
• This fortune celebrates inherent purity and exceptional character. The white lotus emerging from the jade pond symbolizes maintaining integrity in any environment—untainted by surrounding circumstances.
• "Bone structure surpasses the ordinary" suggests you possess rare qualities or talents that naturally elevate you above common struggles. This is about destiny and innate gifts, not manufactured success.
• All sacred meanings are highly favorable without exception. This is a fortune of natural excellence and divine favor. Simply being yourself—maintaining your principles and authenticity—will draw recognition and success.

Thượng Cát (Tốt / Cát)

**Sacred Meaning:**

- **Hôn nhân:** hôn nhân thích hợp

• Tất cả ý nghĩa thiêng đều rất thuận lợi không có ngoại lệ. Đây là quẻ về sự xuất sắc tự nhiên và ơn trời. Chỉ cần là chính mình—duy trì nguyên tắc và tính chân thực—sẽ thu hút sự công nhận và thành công.

### ENGLISH TRANSLATION

Poor Fortune (Unfavorable / Caution)

**Sacred Meaning:**
- **Health:** illness burdens increase
- **Wealth:** not favorable
- **Reputation:** empty blossoms and flowers
- **Travel / Movement:** people face danger
- **Marriage:** not suitable
- **Misfortune:** litigation not fulfilled
- **Blessings:** name and profit not suitable
- **Litigation:** affairs without completion

**Fortune Poem:**
Flowers bloom and flowers thank within the spring breeze,
Venerable matters pass by, although wasting hundreds of years in the middle,
The descendants of children and grandchildren today already miss,
Arriving at the head and making ten thousand affairs become empty.

**Interpretation:**
• This fortune reflects the impermanence of worldly success and the emptiness of material pursuits. "Flowers bloom and thank" captures the fleeting nature of achievements—what flourishes today fades tomorrow.
• The emphasis on "wasting hundreds of years" and "affairs become empty" warns against investing energy in endeavors that ultimately lead nowhere. Current efforts may feel productive but lack lasting substance.
• The remedy is radical reassessment. Stop chasing what appears valuable but proves hollow. Focus instead on what endures—relationships, integrity, spiritual growth. This fortune calls for accepting loss and redirecting toward what truly matters.

### ENGLISH TRANSLATION

Middle Fortune (Moderate / Fairly Auspicious)

**Sacred Meaning:**
- **Health:** gradually improving
- **Wealth:** illness and delay
- **Reputation:** inquire and meditate
- **Travel / Movement:** there is fame and profit
- **Marriage:** need to wait for the right time
- **Misfortune:** marriage suitable
- **Blessings:** extremely noble
- **Litigation:** appropriate and rich

**Fortune Poem:**
A single lifetime of ten thousand affairs suffering and bitter,
Summer and ritual halls seek to begin stopping naturally,
Like meeting autumn and becoming winter until after,
A crane and stork together with the waist and loins become slow.

**Interpretation:**
• This fortune acknowledges prolonged difficulty but signals approaching relief. "Suffering and bitter" describes the past and present, while seasonal progression ("summer to winter") indicates natural cycles bringing change.
• The key message is about timing and endurance. "Begin stopping naturally" suggests effort should shift from active striving to patient waiting. The hard phase is ending, but forcing acceleration will backfire.
• The crane and stork imagery represents grace and longevity after struggle. What slows down (becomes "slow") isn't decline but maturation. Success comes through accepting the current pace rather than resisting it.
